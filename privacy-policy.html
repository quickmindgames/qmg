Privacy Policy

Thank you for playing QuickMind Sudoku! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or contact details.

However, to improve your experience and the quality of our game, we collect certain non-personal information automatically through third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics, such as game progress, features used, device type, and general gameplay behavior. This helps us understand how players interact with the app and identify areas for improvement.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: We use Crashlytics to collect crash reports and error logs. This information is used solely to diagnose and fix technical issues, ensuring a stable and smooth experience.
  • Firebase Remote Config: We use Remote Config to dynamically adjust game parameters (such as difficulty, hints, and features) based on aggregated analytics data. This allows us to make the game easier or more challenging, and to introduce new features without requiring an app update.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Analyze gameplay trends and improve game design.
  • Monitor app performance and fix bugs or crashes.
  • Adjust game features and difficulty dynamically for a better user experience.
  • Ensure the app functions correctly across different devices and Android versions.

We do not use your data for advertising or marketing purposes, nor do we share your data with third parties for such purposes.

3. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. All analytics and crash data are stored securely by Google Firebase and are accessible only to authorized personnel.

4. Children’s Privacy

QuickMind Sudoku is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.

5. Your Choices

You can opt out of analytics data collection by disabling analytics in your device settings (if available) or by contacting us for assistance.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app or on our website.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at:

Email: quickmindgames.dev@gmail.com
